CHEA BUTTA

The Voice of the Block

Chea Butta is a magnetic force in the hip-hop scene, captivating audiences with her raw lyricism and undeniable charisma. Originating from Norfolk, Virginia, Chea Butta has carved out a unique space for herself by blending gritty narratives with catchy, confident beats.

The spotlight is currently on Chea Butta with the release of her highly anticipated new album, “Everybody Love Butta.” The project is a vibrant testament to her growth as an artist, showcasing a diverse range of tracks that explore themes of ambition, life in the city, and unapologetic self-love. Critics and fans alike are praising the album for its tight production and Chea Butta’s most complex and compelling verses to date. “Everybody Love Butta” is a definitive statement that solidifies her position as an essential voice in contemporary music.

Known for her dynamic stage presence and ability to connect instantly with her audience, Chea Butta continues to build a fiercely loyal following. Her dedication to her craft and her authentic storytelling promise a long and impactful career. Keep an eye on Chea Butta—she's not just making music; she's setting the new standard.

TY TOPSHELF

The Architect of Sound

As a founding figure of Topshelf Sounds & Ent., Ty Topshelf is both a gifted artist and a visionary producer. His sound is defined by sharp lyrical wit and atmospheric, genre-bending production that pushes the boundaries of modern hip-hop. He has been instrumental in shaping the careers of many artists on the label.

Known for his magnetic stage presence and ability to deliver infectious hooks, Ty Topshelf is the pulse of the label, constantly innovating and collaborating to define the next wave of music.
