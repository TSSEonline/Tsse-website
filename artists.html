CHEA BUTTA

The Voice of the Block

**Chea Butta** is a powerhouse vocalist and lyricist known for her raw, unfiltered street narratives and powerful delivery. Blending hard-hitting trap beats with introspective storytelling, she captures the realities of urban life with uncompromising honesty. Her breakthrough track, "Block Talk," established her as an undeniable force in the underground scene.

A true artist of the Topshelf brand, Chea Butta doesn't just rap—she speaks for a generation. Her highly anticipated debut album, *Everybody Love Butta*, showcases her versatility and lyrical prowess, proving she is much more than just a rapper; she is a movement.

TY TOPSHELF

The Architect of Sound

As a founding figure of Topshelf Sounds & Ent., **Ty Topshelf** is both a gifted artist and a visionary producer. His sound is defined by sharp lyrical wit and atmospheric, genre-bending production that pushes the boundaries of modern hip-hop. He has been instrumental in shaping the careers of many artists on the label.

Known for his magnetic stage presence and ability to deliver infectious hooks, Ty Topshelf is the pulse of the label, constantly innovating and collaborating to define the next wave of music.
