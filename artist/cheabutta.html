CHEA BUTTA

Biography

Chea Butta is a magnetic force in the hip-hop scene, captivating audiences with her raw lyricism and undeniable charisma. Originating from Norfolk, Virginia, she blends gritty narratives with catchy, confident beats — making her one of the most compelling voices in the region.

Her new album “Everybody Love Butta” showcases her artistic evolution: sharp verses, bold storytelling, and an uncompromising voice that cuts through the noise.

With a dynamic stage presence and a fiercely loyal following, Chea Butta isn’t just making music — she’s setting the new standard.
