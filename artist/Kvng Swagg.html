KVNG SWAGG

The Crown Prince of Trap

Kvng Swagg carries himself with a natural confidence — the kind that comes from surviving the struggle and turning it into drive. His flow is razor sharp, his presence is commanding, and every line he delivers hits with intention.

With viral hits like "Ballin" and "Millions", plus a major collaboration with Slimelife Shawty, Swagg has built his own lane in modern trap. His latest album, Takin' Da Throne, is a declaration: he’s here to dominate the industry, not participate in it.

His newest record, Can’t Say 2 Much, expands his versatility and continues to push his sound into new territory while keeping everything authentically Swagg.
