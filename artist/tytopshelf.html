TY TOPSHELF

The Architect of Sound

Ty Topshelf is more than an artist — he is the visionary mind behind Topshelf Sounds & Ent. As a creator, engineer, and executive, Ty blends raw authenticity with calculated precision, crafting music and developing artists with a style that feels both street-honed and high-end.

Whether he’s shaping melodies, arranging vocals, producing beats, or guiding the TSSE roster, Ty brings a level of intention and discipline that sets the foundation for the entire label. His artistic footprint extends across Norfolk and beyond, making him one of the most influential emerging figures in Virginia's independent scene.

Ty’s latest work, “We Lit,” showcases his creative versatility and knack for making records that resonate in the streets, the clubs, and everywhere between.
