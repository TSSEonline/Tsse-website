KILLAH DAE

The Street Storyteller

Killah Dae is built on authenticity. Every verse he delivers comes straight from experience—real life, real losses, real elevation. His music is unapologetically raw, filled with emotion, hunger, and the scars of what it took to survive.

His breakout project, Pain and Sacrifice, shook listeners with its honesty. He raps with purpose, transforming trauma into art and struggle into scripture. Killah Dae isn’t following trends—he’s documenting the life that shaped him.

His high-energy single Crash Out continues to put his name in new rooms, bringing a harder, more relentless sound that reflects where he stands now in his journey.
